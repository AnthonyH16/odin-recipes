Vegan Lasagna Recipe

Description

Scrumptious lasagna. You wont even know it's vegan.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of lasagna noodles
  • a few jars of sauce
  • Mokoyo vegan mozzarella cheese
  • Violife parmesan cheese
  • Violife Mozzarella Shreds (or any other vegan cheese you'd like)
  • Pack of Beyond Sausage
  • Pack of Beyond Ground "Beef"

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook noodles according to its package instructions
  2. Cook meat according to package instructions
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Farenheit
  4. In oven-safe glass 13" x 9" baking disk, begin layering the ingredients in this order:
    sauce - meat - noodles - cheese
  5. Continue layering in that order until all ingredients have been used
  6. Place in oven for 25 minutes, or until all cheese is completely melted.
  7. When almost finished, broil the top until cheese has reached desired texture
  8. Allow to cool for a while and enjoy!
